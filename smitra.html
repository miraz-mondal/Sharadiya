Santosh Mitra Square


One of the most popular Durga Puja pandals, Santosh Mitra Square in central Kolkata's Lebutala Park is a huge crowd-puller. Organised by BJP councillor Sajal Ghosh, the theme for this year is Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav and the pandal is modelled after the Red Fort. The puja pandal was inaugurated by actor and BJP leader Mithun last week. This year Santosh Mitra Square takes pride in being the costliest puja of Kolkata with a budget of around 18 crore. The cost of the idol itself will be 8 crore. Mintu Pal of Kumartuli who had earlier turned famous by crafting the tallest Durga idol of Kolkata has been assigned to make the Durga of Gold.
